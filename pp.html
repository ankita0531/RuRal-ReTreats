Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

At RURAL RETREATS, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information when you use our services:

  • Personal details (name, email, phone number)
  • Payment information for booking transactions
  • Cookies and usage data for improving our website

3. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used for:

  • Processing bookings and payments
  • Providing customer support
  • Sending promotional offers and travel updates
  • Improving our website experience

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your data. Your information is shared only with:

  • Payment processing companies
  • Travel partners for booking confirmation
  • Legal authorities if required by law

5. Data Security

We implement strong security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to request access, correction, or deletion of your personal data. To do so, email us at support@ruralretreats.com.

7. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Please check this page for the latest version.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about our Privacy Policy, contact us at support@ruralretreats.com.
